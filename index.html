About Gidado FA

Gidado football academy was formed in the year 2016 and privately owned and founded by Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu who is a native of Okelele in Ilorin West Local government area of Kwara-State. Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu was born into Gidado Afase compound in okelele in the year 1967. He is married to Alhaja Rodiat Afase a native of Ilorin.The marriage was bless with six lovely kids...
