About Gidado FA

Gidado Football Academy is a gender-sensitive, non-religious and non-ethnic football Organization situated in Ilorin the capital of Kwara – State.

Gidado football academy was formed in the year 2016 and privately owned and funded by Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu who is a native of Okelele in Ilorin West Local government area of Kwara-State. Alhaji Afase Babatunde Saliu was born into Gidado Afase compound in okelele in the year 1967. He is married to Alhaja Rodiat Afase a native of Ilorin.The marriage was blessed with six lovely kids.

The academy was duly registered with Corporate Affair Commission and also affiliated itself to Kwara-State Football Association at the state level.

Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu is a business morgue who also double as chief executive director of Afase Printing Press and AfaseAutomobile Company Ltd. Due to his philanthropy gesture Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu has supported many community development project within and outside the state.

Before the establishment of his own academy he was the director of KwaraUnited Feeder Football club an amateur team.Alhaji Afase Babatunde Soliu strongly believe in empowering the young talented youth in the society and given them means to end through his love for the game of football.He also encourage the youth to given back to society after reaching their picks.

    Aim and Objectives

    Good Sportmanship and Performance

    Gidado Football Academy aim at promoting good sportsmanship and encourage high stand of performance and conduct on and off the field inclusive of players, coaches and management.

    Maximizing Potentials

    To emphasize each players potential maximize, guilding each of them through a structured and goal oriented process of continuous development.

    Opportunity for Talents

    To provide the opportunity for talented young soccer loving individual train compete high level with reasonable commitment of time, one that provide time for life beside soccer.

    Standardized Football Administration

    To set a landmark for standardize football administration in the country while changing the face of soccer in the country.

    Set standards for Future

    To teach the value of punctuality, good communication, manner, hard work and setting goal for the future.
